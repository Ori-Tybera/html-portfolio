The Best Movies According to Ori

My top 3 movies of all time.

Spiderman: Across the Spiderverse

This movie is fantastic; it has insanely good art direction, each frame is carefully crafted, each character is unique, and the way the entire art style constantly changes is beautiful.

If

This is a beautifully sad story about a girl reliving her childhood while her father undergoes surgery.

Moana

In my opinion, the best disney movie of all time. Moana's journey where she sets sail to have Maui put back the heart, but ends up becoming a wayfinder herself to put back the heart on her own, facing challenges across the sea, is an amazing story.
